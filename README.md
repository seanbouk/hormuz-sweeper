# Hormuz Sweeper

A minesweeper game set in the Strait of Hormuz. Clear the naval mines from one of the world's most strategically important waterways.

## How to Play

- **Click** (or tap) a cell to reveal it
- **Right-click** (or long-press on mobile) to flag a suspected mine
- Numbers show how many adjacent cells contain mines
- Clear all safe water cells to win!

The grid follows the real coastline of the Strait of Hormuz — you can only sweep cells that are in the water. Land cells along the Iranian coast, Musandam Peninsula, and islands are impassable.

## Play

Visit **[seanbouk.github.io/hormuz-sweeper](https://seanbouk.github.io/hormuz-sweeper)** to play.

## About

This is a joke game. The Strait of Hormuz is a narrow waterway between Iran and Oman through which roughly 20% of the world's oil passes. It seemed like a good place to sweep for mines.

Your best completion time is saved locally in your browser.
